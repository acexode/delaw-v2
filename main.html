Is an oral agreement to transfer land enforceable in Nigeria?

Generally no. By s.4 of the Statute of Frauds and the Land Use Act, a contract for the disposition of land must be evidenced in writing. However, the equitable doctrine of part-performance may render it enforceable.

Odutola v. Papersack (Nig) Ltd
(2006) 18 NWLR (Pt. 1012) 470, SC
